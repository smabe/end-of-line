# Adventure Time — Smoke Test Facts

## Characters

- **Finn the Human**, voiced by Jeremy Shada, debuted in 2010 as the last known human in the Land of Ooo and aged in real time across the show's eight-season run.
- **Jake the Dog**, voiced by John DiMaggio, has magical shape-shifting powers that let him grow, shrink, and morph at will — abilities he later passed on to his five pups.
- **Marceline the Vampire Queen**, voiced by Olivia Olson, is over 1,000 years old and plays an axe bass that was originally her family's heirloom battle-axe; she and Princess Bubblegum are a confirmed couple.
- **Princess Bubblegum**, voiced by Hynden Walch, rules the Candy Kingdom and is canonically the series' most scientifically intelligent character, often shown conducting elaborate experiments.
- **BMO**, voiced by storyboard artist Niki Yang (who also voices Lady Rainicorn entirely in Korean), is a sentient video game console who lives with Finn and Jake and starred in a solo origin episode set entirely before the series.
- **Ice King**, voiced by Tom Kenny, was originally a normal human antiquarian named Simon Petrikov whose magic crown gradually drove him mad while also granting him ice powers.
